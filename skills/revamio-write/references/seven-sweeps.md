# Seven Sweeps editing pass

Run each sweep as a separate focused pass. After all seven, score 0–10; ship
only at 8+. If a sweep forces big changes, re-run the earlier sweeps.

1. **Clarity** — would a first-time reader understand every sentence once?
   Cut jargon, shorten, split run-ons.
2. **Voice & tone** — does it sound like the brand (per context file)? Remove
   any never-use words. Match the established energy.
3. **So what** — every claim must answer "why should the reader care?" Convert
   features to ICP benefits.
4. **Prove it** — every assertion needs support (number, example, source).
   Unsupported claims get backed or cut. No fabrication.
5. **Specificity** — replace vague nouns/adjectives with concrete ones. Names,
   numbers, real scenarios beat "powerful", "seamless", "robust".
6. **Heightened emotion** — does the open earn attention? Does the ICP feel
   understood? Strengthen the hook and the stakes.
7. **Zero risk** — remove anything that could read as overclaiming, spammy, or
   off-brand. Reduce reader risk (proof, specificity, honesty).

## Scoring
- 9–10: publish-ready.
- 7–8: one more targeted pass.
- ≤6: structural rework — revisit the anchor and framework.
