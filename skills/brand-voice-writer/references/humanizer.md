# Humanizer pass (final gate before delivery)

Strip AI tells; confirm it reads like a person wrote it. Target human-ness 8+/10.

## Remove these AI tells
- **Overused transitions:** "Moreover", "Furthermore", "Additionally", "In
  today's fast-paced world", "It's worth noting", "That said".
- **AI clichés:** "delve", "tapestry", "navigate the landscape", "unlock",
  "elevate", "game-changer", "in the realm of", "robust solution".
- **Hedging:** "It's important to note", "generally speaking", "in many cases"
  (when not needed).
- **Corporate buzzwords:** "synergy", "leverage" (as filler), "cutting-edge",
  "best-in-class", "seamless" (unless literally true).
- **Robotic patterns:** rule-of-three everywhere, every paragraph the same
  length, "Firstly/Secondly/Thirdly", over-balanced "on one hand/on the other".

## Human-ness signals to add
- Varied sentence length (mix short punchy lines with longer ones).
- Concrete specifics over abstractions.
- A real point of view; mild opinion is fine.
- Contractions and natural rhythm where the voice allows.

## Scoring factors (0–10)
- Reading ease (not overwrought), sentence-length variance, AI-pattern count
  per 1000 words (lower is better), specificity ratio (concrete vs vague).

## Always
- Re-check the never-use vocabulary list from `revamio-context.md`.
- Humanizing must not flatten the brand voice — preserve the established tone.
